Frame
5 min
"This is about work energy, not performance. No wrong answers. What gives you energy at work, what takes it away. Be specific - not 'meetings' but 'meetings where I'm just listening for 60 min with nothing to contribute'."
Silent Write
10 min
Everyone writes 3-5 items per column. One thing per sticky. Green for energizers, red for drains. No talking. Set a visible timer.
Share + Cluster
30 min
Round-robin, ~3 min each. Person walks to the board, sticks their post-its up, explains each one briefly. As they share, group similar themes together. No debate - just listen. One clarifying question max per person.
Spot Patterns
10 min
Step back and look at the clusters together. Ask: "What surprised you? What do multiple people share?" Name the top 3-4 shared energizers and drains. No action items - just awareness.
Close
5 min
One sentence each: "One thing I'll remember from someone else's map." Photograph the board for the record.

Materials

Whiteboard or wall space with two clear columns. Green post-it notes and red post-it notes (or two other distinct colors). Markers. A timer visible to the room. That's it.

Moderator Notes

  • This is the first session of the day. Energy is fresh. Keep it light and warm - it sets the tone for Thursday.
  • Emphasize "work energy" not "skills" or "performance." This is not a competency map. Nobody is being evaluated.
  • If someone writes something vulnerable (e.g., "drains me: feeling out of the loop on decisions"), acknowledge it and move on. Don't fix it in the moment.
  • During share+cluster, physically move stickies into groups as you hear overlap. This makes patterns visible in real-time.
  • Watch the clock on share+cluster. 10 people at 3 min each = 30 min exactly. Gently cut if someone runs long.
  • No action items by design. The value is mutual awareness, not a to-do list. If something actionable surfaces naturally, note it for Friday's action items session.
  • Photograph the board before leaving the room.
