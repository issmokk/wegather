Moderator Notes

  • This is a review session, not a creation session. State current state, get reactions, decide. No deep-dives.
  • If a topic runs long, park it: "We'll take this offline with [name]. Moving on."
  • For each topic, aim for one concrete decision or action item. Not "we should improve X" but "Y will do Z by [date]."
  • After Meeting Cadence (at ~30 min), thank Vish and Nicole and let them leave. The remaining 3 topics are pure engineering.
  • Bring up the pinned Slack message on screen during async norms. People will react to seeing their own behavior reflected.
  • Meeting cadence: have the calendar data ready. People forget which recurring meetings they're in.
  • On-call is last intentionally -- it's mostly a review of what's already set up, and training is out of team's hands.
  • Capture decisions in a shared doc as you go. This feeds into Friday's action items session.
