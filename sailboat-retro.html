Wind
"What propelled us forward?"
Forces that pushed the team in the right direction during Q1. Tailwinds -- things that accelerated delivery, improved morale, or made work easier. These are your strengths to protect and amplify.
Prompt questions: What went better than expected? What process or practice should we keep doing? Which collaboration worked really well? What tooling or infrastructure saved us time?
⚓
Anchor
"What held us back?"
Drag on the team. Things that slowed progress, created frustration, or wasted effort. Unlike rocks (future risks), anchors are problems you already experienced. Focus on systemic patterns, not individual blame.
Prompt questions: Where did we lose the most time? What felt harder than it should have been? Which dependencies blocked us? What recurring problem did we keep hitting?
⚠️
Rocks
"What risks lie ahead?"
Hidden dangers beneath the surface that could wreck you in Q2/Q3. Things the team sees coming but hasn't addressed yet. Could be technical debt, staffing gaps, unclear ownership, or external dependencies.
Prompt questions: What could go wrong with the Q2/Q3 roadmap? Where are we making assumptions we haven't validated? What knowledge is concentrated in one person? What external dependency worries you?
🏝
Island
"Where are we heading?"
The destination -- what success looks like by end of Q3. This grounds the retro in forward motion instead of just reflecting on the past. The island gives context to everything else: the wind is only useful if it pushes you toward this goal.
Prompt questions: What does "done well" look like by September? What would make you proud of this team in 6 months? What's the one thing we must ship? How should the team feel by end of Q3?

Moderator Notes

  • Silent writing is non-negotiable. 9 people in open discussion = 3 people talking. Enforce the quiet.
  • You moderate, you don't participate. No stickies from you. Your job: cluster, timekeep, draw out quiet voices.
  • Start with Wind (positive energy first), then Anchor, then Rocks, then Island.
  • When placing stickies: one person reads theirs (~15 sec), you cluster live. No debate during placement.
  • Dot voting: 3 votes per person across all quadrants. Top 3-4 items get discussed.
  • Actions must be specific: owner + what + when. "Improve communication" is not an action.
  • If Anchor gets heated, redirect: "What system allowed this?" not "Who caused this?"
  • Photograph the board at the end -- feed into Friday's Action Items session.
